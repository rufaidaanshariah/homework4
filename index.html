For this week's homework, we were asked to create a photomontage. We had a huge snowstorm last week, causing loads of questions and worry about when

spring and summer will arrive. Loads of New Yorkers have been yearning for summer and spring to arrive, cause this cold is far to much to bear. I wanted to

capture the yearning for the warm seasons, but how the snow is making it seem like it'll never appear. To showcase this, I wanted to bring the sun, flowers, bees

and put it behind a giant pile of snow. I used Wikipedia Commons to grab some pictures


The techniques I used were the object selection tool, remove background, and saturation to make it like a more cyber-y aesthetic. I used object selection and remove

for all the flowers and snow. I used saturation to make the colors of the flowers and background more faint and gloomy, to capture the hope of the warm seasons slowly drifting.
